<p><strong>Join us as a student volunteer at STACC 2025!</strong>
</p>
<p>Volunteering at STACC provides an excellent opportunity for medical and
nursing students to gain valuable experience, network with healthcare professionals,
and enhance their understanding of current trends and issues in the field.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Key responsibilities for student volunteers may include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Assisting with registration and check-in processes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Guiding attendees to different conference sessions and exhibition areas.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Providing support during workshops and poster presentations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assisting speakers and panelists as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborating with the organizing team to ensure the smooth flow of the
event.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Fill in your details in the <a href="https://for.sg/staccvolunteer" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">STACC volunteer application form.</a>
</p>
