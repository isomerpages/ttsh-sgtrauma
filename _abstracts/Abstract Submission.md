<h2>Submit your abstract <a href="https://form.gov.sg/5f59061be2745e0011c18355" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Abstracts/MG_7865_inPixio.jpg">
</div>
<p>Delegates are invited to submit abstracts for presentation and inclusion
in the conference proceedings. Submitted abstracts must not have been published
before 17 July 2025.</p>
<p>A committee will review the abstracts and decide if they are to be Oral
or Poster Presentations.</p>

<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Trauma and Acute Care Surgery</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Emergency Medicine</p>
</li>

</li>
<li>
<p>Orthopaedic Surgery</p>
</li>

<p>Neurosurgery</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Papers on the following topics are also welcomed:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Basic Sciences &amp; Research</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Innovation &amp; Technology</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Trauma Rehabilitation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Psycho-social Aspects of Care &amp; Long-term Outomes</p>
</li>

<p>Trauma Training &amp; Education</p>
</li>
</ul>
